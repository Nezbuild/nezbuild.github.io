LinkLead — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-06

Summary: LinkLead does not collect, store, or transmit any of your lead data or personal information. Everything stays on your device.

1. What data the extension reads

LinkLead reads the visible text content of LinkedIn people-search result pages that are already displayed in your browser tab. This includes publicly visible profile information such as names, job titles, companies, locations, and profile URLs.

This reading happens locally in your browser. No information is sent to the extension developer or any third party.

2. What data is stored locally

The extension stores the following data in your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local), which never leaves your device:

  • Exported profile URLs — used to skip duplicate exports across sessions
  • Leads you have added to the Lead Basket — until you export or clear the basket
  • Your campaign tag and UI preferences (e.g. selected preset, debug mode)
  • Daily export count — used to enforce the Free plan daily limit
  • Your license key and activation instance ID (Pro users only) — used for daily subscription revalidation

3. External network requests

The extension makes the following external network requests:

  • LemonSqueezy license API — when you activate a Pro license key, or once per day to verify your subscription is still active. Only your license key and a static instance identifier string are sent. No lead data is included.
  • Hunter.io email enrichment (optional) — if you choose to use the Email Enrichment feature and save your own Hunter.io API key, the first name, last name, and company name of each lead are sent directly to Hunter.io using your API key. This data goes to Hunter.io — not to our servers. This feature is entirely opt-in; you can remove your key at any time from the popup.

No analytics, crash reporting, telemetry, or advertising networks are used.

4. Your exported CSV files

CSV files downloaded by the extension are saved directly to your device via the browser's standard download mechanism. The extension developer has no access to these files.

5. No cookies

LinkLead does not use cookies of any kind. It does not read, write, or interact with browser cookies or LinkedIn session cookies.

6. Your responsibilities under data protection law (GDPR / CCPA)

When you export LinkedIn profile data using this extension, you become the data controller for that data under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR and CCPA). You are responsible for ensuring your use of exported data complies with applicable law, LinkedIn's terms of service, and the privacy rights of the individuals whose data you export.

The extension developer is a data processor only in the limited sense of providing the software tool, and bears no responsibility for how exported data is used.

7. Children's privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, an updated version will be published. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
